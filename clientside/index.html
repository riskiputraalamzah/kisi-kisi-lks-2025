How To Play Hexaria

  1. Input Player 1 and Player 2 names.
  2. Select the game level (easy, medium, hard).
  3. Click "New Game" to start the game.
  4. Player 1 (red) takes the first turn.
  5. Click on an empty hexagon to place your hexagon.
  6. Take over opponent's hexagons by placing a higher value hexagon adjacent to them.
  7. The game ends when there are no empty hexagons left.
  8. The player with the highest score wins.
